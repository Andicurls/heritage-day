Knysna


Located in the Western Cape on the northern shore of a large warm-water esturary, Is the beautiful small town of Knysna.
Knysna is known as the "jewel of the garden route"seeing that its is well known for the wooden furniture and oysters found there.

   


Location and climate


Knysna is located in the Western Cape Province ,In the Garden Route Province .

climate

Knysna has an Oceanic Climate characterized by its warm summers and its long and cool winters. Therefore, to have an optimal experience the best time to travel to Knysna would be late winter going into early spring as the temperatures will be mild, perfect for many outdoor activities that can be enjoyed such as sightseeing.

    Zimbali


    Zimbali, one of South Africa's most vibrant and inspiring eco-estate. It is located on the North Coast of KwaZulu-Natal (South Africa), 25 km North of Umhlanga Rocks and boarding on the South of Ballito.

    Zimbali offers not only a stunning natural backdrop but also provides serene and relaxing experiences


    This Provinces Climate



    The weather conditions in Zimbali are characterised by a mild and moderate climate. Presipitation is notable throughout the entirety of the year. Zimbali is positioned within the Southern hemisphere, allowing for warm and pleasant days in Summer. June is the month with the least amount of precipitation and the maximum amount is expected between November and December.

    It is suggested, that you check the weather forecast before embarking on your journey in order to get the most out of your trip.

    Hartbeespoort


    Hartbeespoort also known as Harties is a town in the North West Province suited on the slopes of Magaliesberg mountain and the banks of the Hartbeesport dam.The town consists of holiday homes and permanent residence which are nestled in the Magaliesberg mountain and beautiful view of the surrounding areas.

    It is a collective name of a few smaller towns situated around the hartbeespoort Dam, including Meerhof, Ifafi, Melodie, Schoemansville and kosmos.Hartbeespoort is a popular vacation destination because of its natural beauty, outdoor activities, wildlife amd a range of attractions. Whether you are looking for adventure, relaxation or cultural expriences, Harties has something for you.
